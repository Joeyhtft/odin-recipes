Taco Recipe

About Tacos

Tacos are a great mexican dish that can be dressed up or down.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 packet taco seasoning
  • 8 taco shells
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream

Instructions

  1. Cook Gound beef in skillet
  2. Add taco seasoning and water, simmer for 5 minutes
  3. Warm taco shells in oven
  4. Fill taco shells with beef mixture
  5. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and sour cream
  6. Serve and enjoy!
