About Burritos

Burritos are a delicious Mexican dish that can be filled with a variety of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Flour Tortilla
  • Black Beans
  • Ground Beef
  • Rice
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Salsa
  • Guacamole

Instructions

  1. Cook ground beef on skillet
  2. Warm flour tortilla on stovetop
  3. Layer black beans, rice, and ground beef on tortilla
  4. Add shredded cheese, sour cream, salsa, and guacamole
  5. Wrap tortilla tightly around the filling
  6. Serve and enjoy!
